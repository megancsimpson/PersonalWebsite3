About Me

Born and raised in Toronto, Ontario, I relocated to British Columbia to be closer to the mountains and the lifestyle they inspire. After living in both Vancouver and Victoria, I eventually found my home in Revelstoke, a small ski town where I now live and work. With a background in retail, inventory management, and customer experience, I currently manage Mint Day Spa. In this role, I oversee daily operations, support my team, and ensure the business runs efficiently while delivering a high-quality client experience. I am a natural problem solver with a strong curiosity about how systems function, which led me to pursue an education in software development. I am looking forward to launching my career in the industry.
